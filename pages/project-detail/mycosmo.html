MyCosmo

Be your universe

iOS Swift SwiftData

Overview

What started as a basic school project evolved into something much more meaningful. As I delved deeper into development, I found myself captivated by the possibilities and decided to transform it into a professional, polished application.

Beyond its initial scope, MyCosmo now serves as an educational resource for my school, offering students and faculty a practical example of modern iOS 18 development using Swift, SwiftUI, and SwiftData. The app showcases real-world implementation of Apple's latest technologies while providing valuable astronomical data through integrations with NewsAPI and NASA's APOD API.

Key Features

Space News

Stay updated with the latest discoveries, missions, and breakthroughs in space exploration.

Space Info

Explore daily astronomy pictures and detailed explanations from NASA's APOD, bringing the wonders of space to your fingertips.

Observations

Document your astronomical observations, thoughts, and discoveries with a personal space journal integrated into the app.
