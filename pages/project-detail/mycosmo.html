Overview

A space and astrology app for astronomy enthusiasts. Originally a school project that has evolved into something bigger and more dynamic. Like space itself, it's still growing and holds unknown potential.

Key Features

Daily Astronomy

Explore NASA's astronomy picture of the day with detailed explanations.

Moon Phases

Track lunar phases and celestial events in your area.

Space News

Stay updated with the latest discoveries and space missions.
